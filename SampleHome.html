Our Vision: To be a Community of Believers committed to the Lordship of Jesus Christ and to the Ministry of the Holy Spirit, Welcoming People into the local congregation and Partnering with Ministries and Missionaries around the world for Spreading the Good News of the Kingdom.

Our Mission: We seek to carry out Jesus' great commission by: Finding those who have yet to experience His love and freedom both locally and globally, Cultivating growing relationships with Christ individually and corporately, and Caring for our local church body and the surrounding community.
